Placeholder for application icons.

Add your logo at resources/logo.png (1024x1024) and generate platform icons via:

npm install --save-dev electron-icon-builder
npx electron-icon-builder --input=./resources/logo.png --output=./resources --flatten

This will produce:
- resources/icon.icns (macOS)
- resources/icon.ico  (Windows)
- resources/icon.png  (Linux 512x512)
